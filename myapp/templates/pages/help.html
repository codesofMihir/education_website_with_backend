Frequently asked questions

Accordion-style questions keep the section compact while still showing detailed answers when needed.

How do I reset my student account password? +

Use the “Forgot password” option on the login page, enter your registered email or mobile number, and follow the verification instructions sent by the platform.

Why can’t I submit my assignment file? +

Check the file type, file size, internet connection, and deadline status. You can also provide a small helper note below the upload area for accepted formats and maximum size.

Where can students download certificates or progress reports? +

Place certificate and report downloads inside the student dashboard under progress or achievements, then link to that area from the help page for quick access.

How should I handle live class joining issues? +

Show a checklist for browser permissions, internet speed, meeting link status, and app updates. This reduces repeated support requests and improves user confidence.
